# Tourist Attraction & Destination Page Structures Guide

**Source**: Consolidated reference for building consistent, SEO-friendly tourism pages (e.g., puertovallarta.net). Applied to Top Huatulco (9 bays).

## General Best Practices
- **Hero Section First**: High-quality image/video + H1 + benefit-driven subheadline + prominent CTAs.
- **Mobile-First & Fast Loading**: WebP images, lazy loading, responsive design.
- **Scannable Content**: Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear H2/H3 hierarchy.
- **Multiple CTAs**: Placed throughout the page.
- **High-Quality Visuals**: Authentic photos with descriptive alt text.
- **SEO Foundations**: Keyword-optimized titles/meta, one H1 per page, internal linking.
- **Schema Markup**: Use `TouristAttraction` as base; add specific types (e.g., `Beach`).
- **Trust & Practicality**: Reviews, practical info, honest descriptions.

## 1. Beach / Nature Attraction Page Structure (Primary for Huatulco Bays)

**Recommended Structure**:
1. **Hero Section** — Stunning image/video + H1 + CTAs.
2. Quick Overview / Introduction.
3. Highlights / Why Visit (grid or icon cards).
4. Things to Do & Activities.
5. Location, Access & Map (interactive + parking/accessibility).
6. Practical Visitor Information (best time, facilities, safety, rules).
7. Photo & Video Gallery (masonry/carousel).
8. Reviews & Testimonials.
9. FAQ (accordion).
10. Nearby Attractions & Related Content.
11. Final CTA Section.

**Key Emphasis**: Sensory experience, activities, practical beach-specific info.

## Application Notes for Top Huatulco
- All 9 bay pages follow the Beach/Nature template.
- Hub page (/destinations/) acts as category hub with filterable feel.
- Strong internal linking between bays + itineraries + travel-guide.
- Add practical elements: taxi rates, boat access, snorkel conditions, shade, crowds by season.
- Use consistent card/grid patterns from existing Top El Salvador / Huatulco design.
