About Top Huatulco

Top Huatulco is an independent practical travel guide for visitors planning Huatulco and the nearby Oaxaca coast.

This site focuses on practical planning: which bay to choose, how to get around, when to book a boat, what to pack, where to eat, and how to avoid avoidable friction on a short trip.
