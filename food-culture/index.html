Food & culture

Food and Culture in Huatulco

Where to eat in Huatulco: bay seafood, La Crucecita restaurants, Oaxacan dishes, markets, mezcal, and practical meal timing.

Seafood on the bays

Chahué, Santa Cruz, and Maguey are the easiest places for grilled fish, ceviche, shrimp, and casual palapa lunches.

Oaxacan food in town

La Crucecita is better for tlayudas, mole, mezcal, markets, bakeries, coffee, and non-resort dinners.

How to eat well

Use beach palapas for lunch, town restaurants for dinner, and the market for breakfast fruit, coffee, and snacks.
