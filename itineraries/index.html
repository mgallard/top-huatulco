Trip plans

Huatulco Itineraries

Pick an itinerary by energy level, not just trip length. The best Huatulco plan protects calm mornings, avoids zig-zag routes, and leaves room for sea conditions.

How to choose your plan

First-timers usually need one boat/snorkel morning, one town/market evening, one easy beach day, and one flexible slot. Longer trips can add Copalita, Mazunte, Puerto Ángel, or a second bay without turning the vacation into transport work.

3 days

Arrival, central bay, boat day, and La Crucecita/Copalita choice.

4 days relax + snorkel

Better for families/couples who want real beach time, not a checklist.

5 days Oaxaca coast

Adds west-coast context such as Mazunte or Puerto Ángel without rushing every day.

Snorkel + boat day

A single-day route that keeps lunch, conditions, and return energy realistic.
