Huatulco itinerary

4 Days Relax + Snorkel

Slower pacing for couples or families who want beach time without moving hotels.

Day 1

Check in, swim at your home bay, early dinner.

Day 2

Morning snorkel boat tour; quiet afternoon back at hotel.

Day 3

La Crucecita market, Chahué beach, seafood dinner.

Day 4

Choose San Agustín, Copalita, or a resort day pass.

Have a backup idea

If wind or rain changes the plan, move boat and snorkel activities to the calmest morning and use La Crucecita, Copalita, or a slow seafood lunch as your flexible option.
