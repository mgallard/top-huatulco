Puerto Ángel

A working fishing town west of Huatulco with simple seafood, harbor views, and a slower local feel than the resort bays.

How to use Puerto Ángel

Treat Puerto Ángel as a half-day coast stop, usually paired with Mazunte or Zipolite. Go for lunch, a harbor walk, and a look at everyday coast life rather than polished resort beach infrastructure.

Best time

Late morning to lunch. Return before dark unless you have arranged transport.

Getting there

Taxi or rental car is easiest. Colectivos can work but require more patience.

Pair nearby

Mazunte, Punta Cometa, Zipolite, or a slow return to La Crucecita for dinner.
