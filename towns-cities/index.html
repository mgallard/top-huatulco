Huatulco Towns and Nearby Places

Where to base yourself, eat, shop, take boats, and plan day trips around Huatulco.

La Crucecita

Main square, church, market, Oaxacan restaurants, pharmacies, banks, and mezcal bars. Best base if you want food choices and easy taxis.

Open guide
Santa Cruz

Pier, marina, cruise arrivals, seafood, beach restaurants, and boat-tour offices. Best for arranging bay days.

Open guide
Copalita

Eco-archaeological park, river viewpoint, birding, and cultural context. Best as a half-day break from beach time.

Open guide
Mazunte

Bohemian beach town about 45–60 minutes away with Punta Cometa viewpoint, turtle center context, and sunset crowds.

Open guide
Puerto Ángel

Working harbor, simple seafood, and a quieter counterpoint to Huatulco resort zones.
