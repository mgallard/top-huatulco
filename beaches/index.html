Beach planning

Quick verdict: choose beaches by conditions, not by the prettiest name

Huatulco beaches change by access, shade, food, swell, boat traffic, and time of day. The right choice is usually one easy beach plus one meal plan, not a rushed attempt to sample every bay.

Choose / avoid

  • Choose this when: you know whether the group wants calm swimming, snorkeling, seafood lunch, sunset, or quiet scenery.
  • Avoid this when: you only have a vague “best beach” list; Tangolunda, Chahué, Maguey, Cacaluta, and Chachacual solve different days.
  • Better backup idea: If wind or visibility is poor, switch to La Crucecita, Chahué sunset, Mercado 3 de Mayo, or a pool/rest afternoon instead of forcing a remote beach.

Tangolunda

Largest resort bay. Best for First-timers, families, resort beach days. Taxi from HUX 20–25 min; from La Crucecita 10 min. Best by taxi or hotel shuttle.

Open guide
Chahué

Walkable town beach. Best for Sunsets, local meals, easy evenings. Walk 10–15 min from La Crucecita or short taxi from Santa Cruz.

Open guide
Santa Cruz

Harbor and boat hub. Best for Boat tours, seafood, cruise arrivals. Taxi or walk from La Crucecita; boats leave from the pier.

Open guide
El Maguey

Small snorkel bay. Best for Calm water, casual lunch, short boat trips. Taxi/road access from town or boat stop from Santa Cruz.

Órgano

Quiet neighboring bay. Best for Low-key swimming, fewer vendors. Usually reached by boat as part of a bay tour.

Cacaluta

Remote national park bay. Best for Scenery, clear water, boat-day feel. Boat access is the normal visitor route; hiking access requires local guidance and heat planning.

Chachacual

Protected snorkel stop. Best for Snorkeling, clear water, boat tours. Boat only. Usually included on multi-bay tours from Santa Cruz.

San Agustín

Longer local beach. Best for Beach walking, local weekends, relaxed lunch. Road access is slower and bumpier; boat access depends on sea state.

Conejos

Small upscale bay. Best for Quiet resort edge, rocky viewpoints. Taxi from Tangolunda area or hotel access depending on where you stay.
